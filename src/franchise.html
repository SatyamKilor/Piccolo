Concept & Capacity

  • This model entirely focuses on “A quick coffee run” & “easy to grab quick bytes”
  • Consists of no seating to minimal seating of 2-12 people

SetUp Essentials

  • The expected franchise budget is INR 30,000,00 onwards
  • The minimum area requirement for the model is 250sq.ft
  • This model requires a minimum staff of 4 baristas 2 Housekeeping / Helper.

Beyond The Basics

  • It includes coffee equipments, furniture, store design and other expenses, breakdown of which will be provided after inspections and calculations of scope of work based on area of site. The outer seating/service space’s extra furniture, decor and landscaping charges of area outside of the store premises are to be decided by owner.

Growth & Gains

  • The estimate net profit on sale could be around 35%.
  • Expected Break-even could be around 14-18 months.
